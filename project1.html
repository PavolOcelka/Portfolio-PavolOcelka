MindMate IOS App

Technologies: Swift, SwiftUI, Engine Haptics

MindMate is a mental well-being app designed to help you understand and manage your emotions better. It features tools like box breathing and a sleep calculator for relaxation and better sleep. You can track your mood daily, write a journal, and use the mood dictionary to expand your emotional vocabulary—so you can describe how you feel beyond just "good" or "bad."

Main View

The Main View in MindMate gives you a quick overview of your mental state. Here’s what you’ll find:

  • Mood Tracking – Log your mood daily with dynamic color gradients reflecting your emotions.
  • Journal – Write down your thoughts and feelings, with your latest entry always at the top.
  • Interactive Character – Tap to access tools for stress management and better sleep.
  • Quick Access to Mood Dictionary – Helps you describe your emotions more precisely.

The design features gradient backgrounds, smooth animations, and intuitive navigation for a seamless user experience.

Mood Dictionary

Explore a collection of emotions, each with a brief description to help you better understand various emotional states. This section is designed to provide insights into feelings such as happiness, sadness, anger, anxiety, and more, offering a visual representation through color gradients for a more intuitive experience.

Mood Tracking

The MoodDetailView provides users with a detailed view of their mood entries. Users can create, edit, and delete mood records, each accompanied by a mood category (e.g., Happy, Sad, Angry, etc.). The user interface allows for a title input field, a picker for selecting the mood category, and a text editor to describe the mood in detail.

Features:

  • Mood Input: Users can input a title for their mood entry.
  • Mood Category Selection: A picker is provided to choose a mood category from a list of predefined options.
  • Text Description: Users can write detailed descriptions of their mood.
  • Font Size Adjustment: A slider allows users to adjust the font size of the mood description.
  • Delete Option: Users can delete a mood entry by clicking the trash icon.

Journals

The JournalDetailView provides users with a detailed interface for managing individual journal entries. Users can update the journal title using an input field, view the formatted entry date, and edit their content in an intuitive text editor.

Features:

  • Title Editing: Modify the journal title easily with a prominent text field.
  • Date Display: The entry date is clearly shown for context.
  • Content Editor: A guided text editor helps users record their thoughts and experiences with a placeholder prompt.
  • Font Size Adjustment: A slider allows users to adjust the text size for comfortable reading.
  • Deletion Capability: A delete button enables the removal of a journal entry.

Activities

Stress & Anxiety activity helps manage stress through a simple breathing exercise. Tap to start the cycle:

Inhale: Deep breath in.

Hold: Hold your breath.

Exhale: Slowly release your breath.

For better Sleep, use the Sleep Calculator to find the ideal bedtimes based on your wake-up time.
